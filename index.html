About Me

I’m a senior at the University of California San Diego studying Political Science and Data Analytics. I enjoy using quantitative methods to solve social science problems, and aspire to have a career in data science or intelligence. Currently I'm working on a research project with Professor Michael Joseph which aims to understand the impact of U.S. military intervention of Afghan quality of life. I'm also employed at the UCSD Data & GIS Lab where I'm developing an R Shiny App to automate cleaning and visualization of the library's service statistics data.

In my freetime I love to play chess and train for triathlons. I also have organized and competed in Rubik’s Cube competitions and achieved a ranking of 11th in the world (more on that below!).
